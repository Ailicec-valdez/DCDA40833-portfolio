Lab 4 - Data Visualization in Tableau Public

Reflection

Design Inspiration

My inspiration for my lab06 basemap design was Noah Kahan’s Stick Season album cover. I chose this cover because I hold this album close to my heart and also it contains some songs that are based on his hometown. Since I am making a map of my hometown I thought this album would be fitting. I translated the visual elements through colors and font types. I wanted my map to replicate the different shades of blues and greens. I also liked how natural it made the map look since it replicated actual Earth colors.

Cartographic Challenges

Overall I didn’t have issues with building my basemap. I struggled a bit with the individual color shades but I was able to learn the system pretty quickly. I decided to feature locations that I have spent lots of time in or areas that I believe are pretty popular.

Technical Challenges

I used chatGPT to create my code. Overall it was a smooth experience, up until I had to open and save my html file. I kept getting errors regarding the location of my CSV and files not found. It seems that I had issues locating my files in my download folder, but using chat I was able to move around my files. I also had some issues with my geocode_cache.csv due to an error with spaces. That was mainly an issue with how I was presenting that section of code in my overall system. I was able to fix that by adjusting where to find the folium section.
